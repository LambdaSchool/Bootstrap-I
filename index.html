Developer

Being a developer is about the thrill of the build, the passion that's needed to commit to project after project. The mindset to wrap your head around one fact: all of your limitations, you will systematically overcome each and every one of them. Then you find out the only limits imposed on you are of your own machination. Congrats, you're a developer!

Of course, I could never truly pen what it means to be a developer within one paragraph. The journey, the knowledge, the experiences and the friendships formed transcend anything I could come up with. So hopefully I can convey it over the one medium I know and love: web development!
