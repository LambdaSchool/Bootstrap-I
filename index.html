Rock Chalk, Jayhawk!!

Christina went to graduate school at The University of Kansas where she studied under David Fedele. While there, she was principal flutist and piccoloist of the wind ensemble, orchestra and Helianthus contemporary chamber ensemble. Christina also took on the role of evening classical music announcer at KU's own Kansas Public Radio.

Go Redhawks!

Ms. Kopecky studied at Southeast Missouri State University where she received her BA in Music with minors in Mass Communication and French while studying flute under Paul Thompson. At Southeast, Christina was principal of the Southeast Missouri Symphony Orchestra, principal of the Southeast Missouri Wind Ensemble, piccoloist of the Cape Girardeau Municipal Band and a member of many other music organizations.
