  • The Breeds

    Information about the breeds used for Search and Rescue, Trailing, Tracking, and Cadaver Search is available on the AKC and CKC sites. Bloodhounds are and old breed descended from the Hounds of Hubert and thousands of years old Great Danes or the German Mastiff / Deutsche Dogge as they are called, are also old breed originating in the early part of the Middle Ages.

    Black and Tan Coonhounds are a fairly newer addition to the hunting breeds and there are two types, the AKC/UKC and the AKC. The AKC is considered and American breed and called the Old Fashioned with a taller stance and longer ears while the AKC/UKC is of smaller stature with more prey drive.
